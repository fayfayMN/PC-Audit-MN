PC-Audit Discovery Checklist 2026-02-01
1.  Financial Documentation

  - PC Engagement Letter: To verify the hourly rate and fee split (usually 50/50).
  - Monthly Invoices: All bills received from the PC.
  - Pay Stubs: Your recent pay stubs to calculate the Labor-to-Fee Ratio.
  - Tax Returns: To prove the income disparity between parties for the Economic Burden model.

2.  Legal Documentation

  - Judgment & Decree: The original court order defining the PC's scope.
  - Previous Court Orders: To identify issues already settled by a Judge.
  - PC Decision Documents: Any written "Decisions" or "Orders" issued by the PC.

3.  Communication Logs

  - Email Archive: All correspondence between you and the PC.
  - Correspondence with Opposing Party: To identify "Inquiry" or "Harassment" threads that led to PC billing.

4.  Categorization Key

  - REDUNDANT: Issue settled previously by the court.
  - ADMIN: Scheduling, billing questions, or portal setup.
  - INQUIRY: One-sided questions with no resolution.
  - RESOLUTION: Actual productive mediation or decision-making.
